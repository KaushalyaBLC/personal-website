Parking Lot Management System (CLI)

The Parking Management Application is a user-friendly and efficient command-line utility designed to simplify vehicle entry and exit management in parking lots. This software solution enables users to log vehicle details upon entering the parking lot, records the entry time, and accurately calculates parking fees based on the duration of stay and vehicle type. With a focus on data integrity and ease of use, the application offers a seamless experience for managing parking records, making it an essential tool for optimizing parking lot operations and enhancing overall efficiency.
